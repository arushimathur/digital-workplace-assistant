Learning & Development Policy - American Express Digital Workplace

1. L&D Budget
Every employee receives an annual Learning & Development budget of $2,000. This resets on January 1 each year and cannot be carried forward. The budget covers online courses, certifications, books, conferences, and workshops.

2. Approved Platforms
Employees have free unlimited access to:
  - LinkedIn Learning (available via SSO)
  - Coursera for Business (select courses)
  - AmEx Internal Academy (company-specific training)
  - O'Reilly Learning (technical books and courses)

3. Certification Reimbursement
Certifications from AWS, GCP, Azure, Salesforce, PMP, and other approved vendors are fully reimbursable from the L&D budget. Certification exam fees and prep materials are covered. Employees who leave within 12 months of reimbursement may be required to repay the cost.

4. Conference Attendance
Employees may request to attend external conferences using their L&D budget. All conference attendance requires manager and HRBP approval. Travel and accommodation for conferences are covered under the travel expense policy separately.

5. Internal Training Programs
HR runs quarterly soft-skills workshops on topics like leadership, communication, and inclusive practices. Engineering teams run bi-weekly Tech Talks open to all employees. Mentorship programs are available — employees can apply via the HR portal in February and August.

6. Manager Approval Process
All L&D requests above $500 require manager approval in the Learning portal. Requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks before the course start date. Approved requests are reimbursed after completion and submission of a receipt.

7. Career Development Plans
Every employee should have a Career Development Plan (CDP) set in Workday. CDPs are reviewed during mid-year and year-end performance reviews. Managers are responsible for supporting employees in identifying growth opportunities aligned with business needs.
