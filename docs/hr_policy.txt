HR Policy Guide - American Express Digital Workplace

1. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year. Sick leave is separate with 10 days per year. Leave requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance via the Workday portal. Unused leave up to 10 days can be carried forward to the next year.

2. Performance Review
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year — mid-year in July and year-end in December. Ratings are on a scale of 1 to 5. Employees rated 4 or above are eligible for a performance bonus. All goals must be set in Workday by the end of January.

3. Learning & Development
Each employee has an annual L&D budget of $2000 for courses, certifications, and conferences. Requests must be approved by the manager and submitted via the Learning portal. AmEx also provides free access to LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, and internal training programs.

4. Benefits
Employees are eligible for health, dental, and vision insurance from day one. The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 5% of salary. Employee Assistance Program (EAP) provides free counseling and mental health support.

5. Code of Conduct
All employees must treat colleagues with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind is strictly prohibited. Violations should be reported to HR via the Ethics Hotline, which is available 24/7.

6. Hybrid Work Policy
Employees are expected to be in the office a minimum of 3 days per week. Core office days are Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Remote work on Monday and Friday is permitted with manager approval.

7. Onboarding
New employees complete a 2-week onboarding program. IT equipment is provided on or before Day 1. Buddy programs are assigned to help new hires integrate into the team.
